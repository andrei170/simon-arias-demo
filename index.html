06Common Questions

What people actually ask.

Who is Simon Arias?

+

Simon Arias is a self-made entrepreneur, speaker, and the founder of Arias Agencies — the largest U.S. distribution center for American Income Life. He grew up in Youngstown, Ohio, became the youngest State General Agent in AIL history at 24, and founded Steel City Impact, a Pittsburgh youth development nonprofit, in 2020.

What is Arias Agencies?

+

Arias Agencies is the field organization Simon founded in Pittsburgh in 2008. It is the largest U.S. distribution center within Globe Life's American Income Division, with multiple offices and virtual agents nationwide. The agency serves working families with supplemental life insurance.

Is Simon Arias a legitimate entrepreneur?

+

Yes. Simon has led Arias Agencies for nearly two decades, has been named State General Agent of the Year twice, sits on AIL's Executive Council, and runs documented community programs — Steel City Impact (2020), the Inspiring Minds Youngstown chapter (Board Chair since 2015), and The Red Zone with former Ohio State and NFL athlete Maurice Clarett.

What does Steel City Impact do?

+

Steel City Impact is a Pittsburgh nonprofit founded by Simon in 2020. It runs tutoring, academic enrichment, mentorship, field trips, family events, and community service days for local youth. Funds are raised through events including the Annual Celebrity Athlete Cigar Bar gala.

Where is Simon Arias based?

+

Born and raised in Youngstown, Ohio. Founded Arias Agencies in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, where Steel City Impact is rooted. Currently splits time between Pittsburgh and Miami, with the broader Arias Agencies operation running virtually nationwide.

Does Simon do speaking engagements or take mentorship?

+

Yes — keynotes for corporate events, sales kickoffs, leadership offsites, and schools, plus a limited number of 1:1 and cohort mentorship engagements each year. Inquiries via this site or directly to simonarias@ariasagencies.com.
